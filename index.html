Juan Crisóstomo Ibarra y Magsalin (Ibarra)
  • Juan Crisóstomo Ibarra y Magsalin, commonly known as Ibarra, is a Filipino who has returned to his homeland after studying in Europe.
  • He is the son of Don Rafael Ibarra, a wealthy landowner who was unjustly accused and died in prison.
